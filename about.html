About RNHS

Welcome to RNHS, Rosales National High School! Our school is a dynamic and thriving educational institution that is committed to providing quality education to our students. With a team of dedicated and experienced educators, we strive to create a positive and nurturing learning environment that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and social development.

Our school is equipped with modern facilities and resources that support our students' academic and extracurricular activities. We offer a wide range of academic programs, including STEM, humanities, social sciences, and vocational education, to cater to the diverse needs and interests of our students.

At RNHS, we also emphasize the importance of developing our students' character and values. We believe that education is not only about acquiring knowledge and skills but also about cultivating a sense of responsibility, integrity, and empathy. Therefore, we encourage our students to participate in community service and leadership activities that promote social responsibility and civic engagement.

We are proud to be part of the RNHS community, where excellence, innovation, and service are our guiding principles. We invite you to join us in our mission of shaping the minds and hearts of our future leaders.
