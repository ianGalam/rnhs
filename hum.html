HUMSS

The HUMSS strand or Humanities and Social Sciences strand is one of the strands offered in the senior high school (SHS) program in the Philippines. This strand is designed for students who are interested in pursuing careers in fields such as social sciences, education, communication, and the arts.

The HUMSS strand offers a wide range of subjects that focus on human behavior, society, culture, and arts. It includes subjects such as history, literature, social science, psychology, anthropology, and political science. It also offers specialized subjects such as creative writing, journalism, research, and media and information literacy.
